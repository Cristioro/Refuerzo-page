Tarea 18

Text:

“A group of students discussed online education. Carlos said it was easier because he saved time and money. Laura said it was harder because she felt isolated. A survey showed that 70% of students found online learning more stressful than face-to-face classes. However, another study from 2022 indicated that students who studied online got better results in final exams compared to those in traditional classrooms. Finally, teachers argued that online education requires more discipline, but also offers more opportunities.”
Instruction:
Write down 3 different points of view from the text.

  • a) Explain in 10 lines which argument you think is stronger and why, making connections between stress, performance, and opportunities.
  • b) Use at least 2 comparatives (e.g., “more effective”, “less stressful”) and 1 superlative (e.g., “the most convincing”).
